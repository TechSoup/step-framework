# STEP Framework — Strengthening and Tiered Evaluation Process

> A shared, open standard for civil society organization (CSO) due diligence and capacity assessment.

---

STEP helps civil society organizations demonstrate their capacity to funders — once, in a reusable format — while giving those same organizations a diagnostic roadmap for building capacity over time. The "S" in STEP is Strengthening — it comes first deliberately.
