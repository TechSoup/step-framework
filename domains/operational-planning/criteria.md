# Operational Planning and Continuity — Assessment Criteria

This domain assesses whether the CSO plans strategically, ensures financial
sustainability, and has mechanisms for organizational continuity.

---

## Tier 1 — Basic (2 minimum requirements)

- **OPC-B01**: Actively seeks funding to ensure its sustainability (even if it has not yet been successful).
- **OPC-B02**: Has at least one mechanism to measure how it is successfully achieving its mission.

---

## Tier 2 — Foundation (2 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1 requirements, plus:*

- **OPC-F01**: Has a written strategic plan (may be informal, such as documented in meeting minutes).
- **OPC-F02**: Has at least two mechanisms to measure how it is successfully achieving its mission.

---

## Tier 3 — Agency (4 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-2 requirements, plus:*

- **OPC-A01**: Has some method of ensuring that it obtains value for money.
- **OPC-A02**: Produces an annual report in some form.
- **OPC-A03**: Is able to articulate a financial reserves policy, and/or a method of ensuring it has adequate working capital ratio to remain sustainable in the event of financial losses (may be provided as narrative response in questionnaire or as statement in financial manual, for example).
- **OPC-A04**: Has experience preparing and submitting timely grant reports on a regular basis, and storing all supporting documents in an accessible location (identifiable and relatively easily retrieved as required).

---

## Tier 4 — Plus (3 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-3 requirements, plus:*

- **OPC-P01**: Has some kind of succession plan.
- **OPC-P02**: Has codified guidelines or a formal policy on financial reserves/working capital.
- **OPC-P03**: Has experience managing large-scale projects funded by large donors, including experience preparing and submitting timely grant reports on a regular basis, maintaining separate accounts on its books for individual projects that have dedicated funding streams, and a procedure to determine whether it will be able to comply with grantor's requirements before signing any contract with the grantor.

---

## Red Flags

- **Possible red flag** if it has experienced asset misuse in managing a grant, *unless adequate steps were taken after the fact to prevent repeat.*
