# Working with Implementing Partners — Assessment Criteria

This domain assesses whether the CSO exercises appropriate due diligence and
oversight over its downstream implementing partners.

---

## Tier 1 — Basic (1 minimum requirement)

- **IMP-B01**: Has at least have a contract in place for each downstream partner relationship (if the organization has downstream partners).

---

## Tier 2 — Foundation (3 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1 requirements, plus:*

- **IMP-F01**: Conducts some level of due diligence on each downstream partner.
- **IMP-F02**: Has some mechanism to ensure that its downstream partners have the financial capacity to deliver.
- **IMP-F03**: Has some mechanism to ensure that its downstream partners work effectively, respectfully, and transparently.

---

## Tier 3 — Agency (4 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-2 requirements, plus:*

- **IMP-A01**: Takes at least two different steps in conducting due diligence of downstream partners.
- **IMP-A02**: Requires that its downstream partners agree to its applicable policies around safeguarding, data protection, anti-discrimination, anti-fraud, anti-money laundering, and anti-terrorism.
- **IMP-A03**: Has some mechanism to help downstream partners maintain and build capacity.
- **IMP-A04**: Maintains a documented and clear supply chain for the flow of funds and goods/services from a funder, through the organization, through its downstream partner(s) to the ultimate beneficiaries.

---

## Tier 4 — Plus

*No additional minimum requirements beyond Tier 3 for this domain.*
