# Governance — Assessment Criteria

This domain assesses whether the CSO has legitimate, functional governance
structures that provide oversight, accountability, and strategic direction.

---

## Tier 1 — Basic (2 minimum requirements)

- **GOV-B01**: Has governing documents of some sort.
- **GOV-B02**: Is organized as a nonprofit (i.e., no private benefit), or has an adequate explanation for why it is not, and how it otherwise protects its assets.*

> *Requirement may be waived if organization satisfies additional assurance questions (AAQs) providing a legitimate basis for its variance from this requirement.

---

## Tier 2 — Foundation (4 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1 requirements, plus:*

- **GOV-F01**: Has at least 3 board members.
- **GOV-F02**: Board meets at least once per year.
- **GOV-F03**: Board documents its decisions.
- **GOV-F04**: Has some method of addressing potential conflicts of interest, whether or not it is in an independent policy.

---

## Tier 3 — Agency (2 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-2 requirements, plus:*

- **GOV-A01**: Has a written policy in place that governs potential conflicts of interest.
- **GOV-A02**: Has at least one board member with relevant expertise (which may be specific to the organization's programs, to the populations served, or to the regions in which it operates, for example).

---

## Tier 4 — Plus (2 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-3 requirements, plus:*

- **GOV-P01**: Both seeks to ensure diversity on its Board and reflects some aspect of such diversity on the board (economic, racial, ethnic, representative of communities served, etc.)
- **GOV-P02**: Has a written code of ethics or equivalent.

---

## Red Flags

- **Possible red flag** if any board member, officer, or key employee has been charged with a criminal offense or appeared on a terrorist watch list, *unless there was an adequate reason/explanation (such as, it was a mistake).*
- **Possible red flag** for excessive board member/officer/key employee compensation. Excessive compensation would be anything greater than 10% of the approximate annual budget (shown on profile/cover page), *absent a compelling explanation.*
