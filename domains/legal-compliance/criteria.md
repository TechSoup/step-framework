# Legal Compliance — Assessment Criteria

This domain assesses whether the CSO complies with applicable laws, maintains
appropriate management structures, and upholds labor standards.

---

## Tier 1 — Basic (1 minimum requirement)

- **LEG-B01**: Complies with applicable local laws or has a sufficient reason for not doing so.

---

## Tier 2 — Foundation (3 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1 requirements, plus:*

- **LEG-F01**: Has a clear management structure or organogram.
- **LEG-F02**: Provides a safe workspace for its staff and volunteers.
- **LEG-F03**: Complies with relevant labor laws, including minimum wage (absent a reasonable explanation to the contrary).

---

## Tier 3 — Agency (4 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-2 requirements, plus:*

- **LEG-A01**: Has an *internal* whistleblower policy or similar written process.
- **LEG-A02**: Has a human resources policy/manual, or guidebook.
- **LEG-A03**: Has an easily accessible (paper and/or electronic) database or collection of all of its contracts.
- **LEG-A04**: Has a process for determining and approving salaries.

---

## Tier 4 — Plus (3 minimum requirements)

*All Tier 1-3 requirements, plus:*

- **LEG-P01**: Has a document/data retention policy.
- **LEG-P02**: Has either licensed lawyers on staff who advise it on compliance or periodically consult with outside lawyers.
- **LEG-P03**: Clearly and transparently posts its compliance with labor laws for all of its staff and volunteers to see and become familiar with.

---

## Red Flags

- **Possible red flag** if not paying minimum wage (*absent a legitimate explanation or exemption*).
- **Possible red flag** if they have lost or settled lawsuits, *unless there was an adequate response to prevent it from recurring.*
